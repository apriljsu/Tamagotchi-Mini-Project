Game Rules

Your pet has 3 life stats indicators

  • Hunger (1-10 scale)
  • Sleepiness (1-10 scale)
  • Boredom (1-10 scale)

To keep your pet alive, you need to make sure none of the three stats reaches 10 or 0. You can do so by clicking the interactive buttons.
