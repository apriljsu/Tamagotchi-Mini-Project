Game Rules

Your pet has 3 life stats indicators

  • Hunger (1-10 scale)
  • Sleepiness (1-10 scale)
  • Boredom (1-10 scale)

To keep your pet alive, you need to make sure none of the three stats reaches 10. You can do so by clicking the buttons to feed,turn the light off or play with them.
